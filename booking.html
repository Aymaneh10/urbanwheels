    FAQ Section

    General Information

    • What are your hours of operation?

      Our rental locations are open from 8 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Saturday. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

    • How can I contact customer service?

      You can reach our customer service team by calling (123) 456-7890 or emailing support@UrbanWheels.com. Our support hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday.

    • Where are your rental locations?

      We have rental locations in major cities across the country. Visit our Locations page for a complete list and addresses.

    Booking and Reservations

    • How do I make a reservation?

      You can make a reservation online through our website, by calling our reservation line at (123) 456-7890, or by visiting one of our rental locations.

    • Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

      Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team. Please note that changes and cancellations may be subject to fees and availability.

    • What information do I need to provide when booking a vehicle?

      You will need to provide your driver's license, a valid credit card, and contact information. Additional information may be required for specific rental agreements.

    Rental Policies

    • What are the age requirements for renting a car?

      The minimum age to rent a vehicle is 21. Renters aged 21-24 may be subject to a young driver surcharge. Certain vehicle categories may have higher age requirements.

    • What forms of payment do you accept?

      We accept major credit cards including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Debit cards and cash are not accepted for the initial rental deposit.

    • Is there a mileage limit on rentals?

      Most of our rentals come with unlimited mileage, but some special offers and vehicle categories may have mileage limits. Please check your rental agreement for specific details.
